How to Play Block Blast Arena

  1. Drag a block from the tray onto the 9x9 grid.
  2. Cells glow green where it fits, red where it doesn't.
  3. Release to drop it into a valid spot and score points.
  4. Blast any full row, column, or 3x3 box to clear it — chain clears for combo bonuses.
  5. Watch for the 💣 bomb piece — placing it blasts a 3x3 area instantly.
  6. Use your one Undo per game to recover from a bad drop.
  7. Unlock achievements as you score points, chain combos, and use bombs.
  8. The game ends when none of your 3 tray pieces fit anywhere on the board.
