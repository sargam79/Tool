🧠 Understanding Face Compatibility: The Science Behind the Fun

While our AI face compatibility test is designed primarily for entertainment, it's built on real principles from facial analysis research. Studies in evolutionary psychology suggest that facial symmetry and proportion play roles in perceived attractiveness and compatibility. Our algorithm analyzes:

  • Facial Symmetry: How balanced and proportional each face is

  • Feature Correspondence: How facial features complement each other between two faces
  • Expression Analysis: Smile patterns and emotional expression compatibility

Remember, true relationship compatibility depends on much more than facial features — communication, values, and emotional connection matter most. Use this tool for fun, not as a relationship decision-maker!
