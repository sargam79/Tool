Hashtag Generator Best Practices

Instagram Hashtag Tips

Instagram allows up to 30 hashtags per post. Use all 30 for maximum reach. Mix popular hashtags (over 1M posts) with niche hashtags (under 100K posts) for best results.

TikTok Hashtag Strategy

TikTok has no strict hashtag limit, but 5-15 relevant hashtags typically perform best. Include trending #FYP and #ForYou tags for algorithmic visibility.

Twitter Hashtag Guide

Twitter recommends 1-2 hashtags per tweet. Quality over quantity is key on Twitter, as too many hashtags can look spammy.

Hashtag Research

Research competitor hashtags, check hashtag volume and competition, and stay updated on trending tags in your niche for maximum impact.
