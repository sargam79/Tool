What is Resume Score AI?

Resume Score AI is a free ATS resume checker tool that helps job seekers optimize their resumes for applicant tracking systems. Most companies use ATS software to filter resumes, and if your resume is not optimized, it may never reach a recruiter.

Why Resume Score Matters

A high resume score means your resume matches job requirements and contains the right keywords. This increases your chances of passing ATS filters and getting interviews.
