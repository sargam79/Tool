About PNG Converter

Convert your images to PNG format instantly. All conversions happen locally on your device - no uploads to our servers. Perfect for saving images with transparency, resizing, and optimizing for web use.

Why Convert to PNG?

✓ Supports transparency (alpha channel)

FAQ

Is my image processed securely?

Yes! All image processing happens in your browser. We never upload or store your images on our servers.

What formats can I convert?

JPG, GIF, BMP, WebP, and more. Any format your browser supports can be converted to PNG.

Will transparency be preserved?

If your original image has transparent areas, PNG will preserve them. Solid background images will convert with a white background.

What's the file size limit?

We support images up to 10MB. Larger files may take longer to process.
