How to Play Shadow Match

  1. Drag with one finger (or left-click drag) to rotate the 3D object.
  2. Two fingers (or right-click drag) rotates the camera around the scene; pinch or scroll to zoom.
  3. Watch the live shadow on the ground and compare it to the target silhouette in the corner.
  4. The level completes automatically once your shadow's accuracy crosses the match threshold.
  5. 100% match earns 3 stars, 95%+ earns 2, 90%+ earns 1.
  6. Double-tap (or the Reset button) returns the object to its starting rotation. Use Hint to nudge it toward the answer, or Undo to take back your last rotation gesture.
