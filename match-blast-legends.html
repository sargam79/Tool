How to Play Match Blast Legends

  1. Swap two adjacent gems to line up 3 or more of the same color.
  2. Matches clear automatically, and tiles above cascade down to fill the gaps — chains keep scoring.
  3. Match 4 in a line to create a Line Bomb that clears a whole row or column.
  4. Match 5 in a line to create a Rainbow Bomb — swap it with any gem to clear every gem of that color.
  5. Match in a T or L shape to create a Wrapped Bomb that blasts a 3x3 area.
  6. Combine two special bombs by swapping them together for an even bigger blast.
  7. Break Ice and Locked tiles with nearby matches; Stone blocks need a bomb.
  8. Use Hammer, Shuffle, Hint, Extra Moves, Freeze, or 2x Score power-ups if you get stuck.
