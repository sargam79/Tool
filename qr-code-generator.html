FAQ

How do I use a QR code?

Users can scan QR codes with any smartphone camera or QR code reader app. The code redirects to your URL or displays your text.

Can I customize the QR code?

Our tool generates standard QR codes that work with all scanners. Size adjustments can be made by right-clicking the image and saving at desired dimensions.

How long do QR codes last?

QR codes are permanent. As long as the destination exists, the QR code will work indefinitely.

Is this secure?

Yes! QR codes are generated locally in your browser. We never store or transmit your data.
