How to Play Candy Galaxy

  1. Swap two adjacent candies to line up 3 or more of the same type.
  2. Matches clear automatically and trigger cascades as candies fall to fill the gaps.
  3. Each level has a move limit and a score target — reach it before you run out of moves.
  4. Clearing the target earns you 1 to 3 stars based on how far past it you score.
  5. Use Shuffle to reset the board or Hint to reveal a valid move if you get stuck.
  6. Toggle music and sound from the buttons above the title.
