Understanding Your Fertility

Ovulation

Ovulation typically occurs 14 days before your next period. The egg lives for 12-24 hours after release.

Fertile Window

Your fertile window includes 5 days before ovulation plus the ovulation day. Sperm can survive up to 5 days.

Signs to Watch

Basal body temperature rise, egg-white cervical mucus, mild cramping (mittelschmerz), and increased libido.
