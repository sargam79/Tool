What is ToolPond?

ToolPond is a collection of free online calculators and productivity tools built for people who need quick, accurate answers without the hassle. Every tool runs directly in your browser — your data never leaves your device.

I started building these tools in 2026 because I was frustrated with online calculators that demanded my email, showed pop-up ads, or locked basic features behind paywalls. As a web developer based in Pakistan, I decided to create something better: tools that are genuinely free, respect your privacy, and actually work correctly.

Each calculator uses established formulas from textbooks and verified sources. I cross-check results against multiple references before publishing. While these tools are great for quick estimates, always consult a professional for medical, financial, or legal decisions.

What Makes Us Different

  • 100% Free: No subscriptions. No premium tiers. No hidden costs.
  • Privacy-First: All calculations happen locally in your browser. We don't have servers processing your data.
  • No Signup Required: Click and use immediately. No email, no password, no account.
  • Accurate Formulas: Health calculators use WHO standards and peer-reviewed equations. Financial tools use standard industry formulas.
  • Mobile-Friendly: Works perfectly on phones, tablets, and desktops.
