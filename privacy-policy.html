Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Introduction

Shahid Abbas operates the ToolPond website at toolpond.org. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use our website and tools.

We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about how we handle information.

2. Information We Don't Collect

This is our core principle: We do not collect, store, or transmit your personal data.

  • All calculations and processing happen locally in your browser
  • We do not store any input data on our servers
  • We do not track individual users or sessions
  • We do not use cookies to identify you
  • We do not share your data with third parties

3. How Our Tools Work

Client-Side Processing

All ToolPond tools process data entirely in your browser using JavaScript. No information is sent to our servers. This means:

  • Your data never leaves your device
  • Processing is completely private
  • Tools work offline once loaded
  • No account or login required

4. Technical Information

Our hosting provider may automatically collect some technical information when you visit our website:

  • IP address
  • Browser type and version
  • Device information
  • Pages visited
  • Referring website

This information is handled by our hosting provider and is used solely for security and website performance. We do not have access to individual user data from these logs.

5. Cookies

ToolPond does not use tracking cookies. We may use:

  • Essential cookies for website functionality (if any)
  • Local storage for user preferences (stored only on your device)

We do not use cookies for advertising or individual tracking.

6. Third-Party Services

Some tools may use open-source JavaScript libraries loaded in your browser. These libraries:

  • Process data locally in your browser
  • Do not transmit your data to external servers
  • Are only loaded when you use those specific tools

7. Children's Privacy

ToolPond does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. Our tools are designed to be safe for all ages. Since we do not collect personal data, there is no stored information to delete.

8. Contact Form Data

When you contact us through our contact form, the information you provide is handled as follows:

  • Your message is sent directly to our email inbox
  • No data is stored on our website servers
  • We use your email only to respond to your inquiry
  • We do not share your contact information with anyone

9. Security

We implement standard security measures:

  • SSL/TLS encryption (HTTPS) for all pages
  • Regular updates to website code

Since we do not collect or store personal data, the risk of data breaches is minimal.

10. Your Rights

Since we do not collect personal data:

  • There is no data to access, modify, or delete
  • There is no tracking to opt out of

If you have any concerns, you may contact us using the information below.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as needed. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Your continued use of ToolPond constitutes acceptance of the current policy.
