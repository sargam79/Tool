Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026

1. Introduction

ToolBox Pro ("we," "us," "our," or "Company") operates the ToolBox Pro website. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding data collection, use, and disclosure when you use our website and tools.

We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about how we handle information.

2. Information We Don't Collect

This is our core principle: We do not collect, store, or transmit your data.

  • All text processing happens locally in your browser
  • We do not store any input data on our servers
  • We do not track individual users or sessions
  • We do not use cookies to identify you
  • We do not share your data with third parties

3. How Our Tools Work

Client-Side Processing

All ToolBox Pro tools process data entirely in your browser using JavaScript. No information is sent to our servers. This means:

  • Your data never leaves your device
  • Processing is completely private
  • Tools work offline if needed
  • No account or login required

4. Technical Information

We may automatically receive some information when you visit our website:

  • IP address (for security and analytics)
  • Browser type and version
  • Device information
  • Pages visited (for improving our service)
  • Referring website

This information is used solely to improve our website performance and is not linked to any personal identity.

5. Cookies

ToolBox Pro uses minimal cookies, only for:

  • Website functionality
  • User preferences (stored locally)
  • Analytics (aggregated, anonymized)

We do not use cookies to track or identify individuals.

6. Third-Party Services

Some of our tools may use third-party libraries (like QR code generators) that are loaded in your browser. These libraries:

  • Process data locally in your browser
  • Do not transmit your data to external servers
  • Are only loaded when you use those specific tools

7. Children's Privacy

ToolBox Pro does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. Our tools are designed to be safe for all ages. If we become aware of data from a child under 13, we will take immediate action to delete it.

8. Contact Form Data

When you contact us through our contact form, the information you provide is handled as follows:

  • Your message is sent directly to our email system
  • No data is stored on our website
  • We use your email only to respond to your inquiry
  • We will never share your contact information

9. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect our website:

  • SSL/TLS encryption for website transmission
  • Regular security audits
  • Best practices for data handling
  • HTTPS for all website pages

However, since we don't collect data, there's minimal risk of data breaches.

10. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data (we don't store any)
  • Delete your data (nothing to delete)
  • Opt-out of tracking (we don't track individuals)
  • Request a copy of our privacy practices

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes by updating the "Last Updated" date. Your continued use of ToolBox Pro constitutes your acceptance of this Privacy Policy.

12. GDPR & Data Protection

ToolBox Pro complies with:

  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)
  • Other applicable privacy laws

Since we don't collect personal data, most privacy regulations don't apply, but we maintain compliance standards.
