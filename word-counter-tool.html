What is a Word Counter?

A word counter is a free online tool that instantly counts the number of words, characters, sentences, and paragraphs in any text. It's perfect for writers, students, and professionals who need accurate statistics about their content.

How to Use This Word Counter

  1. Paste or type your text in the input box above
  2. Watch real-time statistics update instantly
  3. See word count, character count, and more
  4. Copy your stats or clear to start over

Why You Need a Word Counter

  • Academic Writing: Meet essay word count requirements
  • SEO Content: Optimize content length for search engines
  • Social Media: Check character limits before posting
  • Professional Writing: Track content for articles and reports
  • Time Management: Estimate reading time

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this completely free?+
Yes, completely free. No hidden charges or subscriptions.
Do you store my text?+
No, everything happens locally in your browser. Your text is never sent to servers.
Does it work on mobile?+
Absolutely! Works perfectly on all devices including phones and tablets.
How is reading time calculated?+
Based on 200 words per minute, the average adult reading speed.
