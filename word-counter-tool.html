Frequently Asked Questions About Word Counting

What is the difference between words and characters?
Words are individual units of text separated by spaces, while characters include all letters, numbers, punctuation marks, and spaces. For example, "Hello World" is 2 words but 11 characters. Our word counter displays both metrics to give you complete text analysis.
How does the reading time calculation work?
Reading time is calculated based on the average reading speed of 200 words per minute (a standard metric in publishing and content creation). We divide the total word count by 200 to estimate how long it takes to read your content aloud. This helps content creators plan appropriate content length for their audience.
