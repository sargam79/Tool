How to Play AquaSort

  1. Tap a bottle to lift it as your pour source — it glows and tilts.
  2. Tap another bottle to pour into it.
  3. Pouring only works if the destination is empty or its top color matches.
  4. Consecutive same-color layers pour together automatically in one move.
  5. Illegal pours are blocked with a shake — no liquid moves.
  6. A bottle is solved once it's empty or full of one color.
  7. Clear every bottle to win — fewer moves and less time earns more stars and coins.
  8. Use Undo, Hint, or +Bottle if you get stuck.
