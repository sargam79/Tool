About Penalty Duel

Penalty Duel is a free online penalty shootout game. Drag to aim, hold to charge your shot's power, and release to strike — then switch sides and dive to save the CPU's kicks before time runs out. Play a full best-of-5 shootout with sudden death, three difficulty levels, and a saved win streak. Runs instantly in your browser on desktop or mobile, no download required.
