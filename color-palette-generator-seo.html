Color Theory & Design Tips

Understanding Color Harmony

Color harmony refers to the combination of colors that create an aesthetically pleasing appearance. Our generator uses complementary, analogous, and triadic color relationships for optimal results.

HEX vs RGB vs HSL

HEX colors (e.g., #FF5733) are perfect for web design and CSS. RGB values work best in digital design software. HSL represents color using Hue, Saturation, and Lightness for intuitive color adjustment.

Contrast & Accessibility

Ensure sufficient color contrast for text readability and accessibility. Light colors work best on dark backgrounds and vice versa.

Color Psychology

Different colors evoke different emotions and meanings. Red represents energy, blue suggests trust, green implies growth, and yellow conveys happiness.
