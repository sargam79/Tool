Understanding YouTube CPM & RPM

What is CPM?

CPM (Cost Per Mille) is the amount advertisers pay per 1,000 video views. Higher quality content commands higher CPM rates.

What is RPM?

RPM (Revenue Per Mille) is what you actually earn per 1,000 views after YouTube takes its cut (typically 45%).

CPM by Niche

What's a good CPM rate?

Average CPM is $5-$10. Premium niches like finance can reach $25+. Entertainment typically sees $2-$5.

How much do YouTubers actually earn?
