💡 How to Use Hashtags Effectively
  • Mix trending & niche hashtags: Use 20-30% trending hashtags for visibility, 70-80% niche for relevance
  • Research hashtag volume: Best hashtags have 10K-1M posts (not too popular, not too small)
  • Platform best practices: Instagram: 20-30 hashtags | TikTok: 5-10 | Twitter: 2-3 | LinkedIn: 3-5
  • Placement matters: Instagram: Use in first comment or caption. TikTok: Put in caption. Twitter: Include naturally
  • Timing: Post when your audience is most active (usually 9-10 AM, 1-2 PM, 7-9 PM)
  • Monitor performance: Track which hashtags drive engagement and adjust your strategy
