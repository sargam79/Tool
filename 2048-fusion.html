How to Play 2048 Fusion

  1. Swipe, use arrow keys, or WASD to slide every tile in one direction.
  2. Tiles with the same number merge into one tile with double the value.
  3. A new tile (2 or 4) appears after every move that shifts the board.
  4. Reach 2048 to win — then keep playing for a higher score if you like.
  5. The game ends when the board is full and no merges are possible.
  6. Use Undo, Hint, Shuffle, or Destroy Tile power-ups if you get stuck.
  7. Try Timed or Zen mode, or the seeded Daily Challenge, for a different pace.
