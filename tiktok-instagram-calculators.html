TikTok Money Calculator Guide

How TikTok Pays Creators

TikTok Creator Fund pays $0.02-$0.04 per 1,000 views. Requirements: 10,000 followers, 100,000 views in 30 days. Sponsored content pays significantly more ($500-$5,000+ per post).

Instagram Engagement Calculator Guide

Instagram Influencer Rates

Micro Influencers (10K-100K): $100-$1,000 per post. Mid-tier (100K-1M): $1,000-$10,000. Macro Influencers (1M+): $10,000-$100,000+.

Creator Economy 101

Monetization Strategies

AdSense/Creator Fund - Passive income from ads. Sponsorships - Brands pay for product placement. Affiliate Marketing - Commission from product sales. Merchandise - Sell branded products. Patreon/Subscriptions - Fan support platform.

FAQ

How accurate are these calculations?

These are estimates based on industry averages. Actual earnings vary by niche, audience location, and content type.

Can I make money on social media?

Yes! Multiple platforms offer monetization: TikTok, Instagram, YouTube, Twitch. Start by building authentic audience.

What niche pays the most?

Finance, tech, and business content typically commands highest CPM rates. Entertainment has larger audiences but lower rates.
