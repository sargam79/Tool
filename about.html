About Us

Free online calculators built with accuracy and privacy in mind

Who We Are

ToolPond was founded in 2026 by Shahid Abbas, a web developer based in Pakistan. The main purpose of this website is to provide a comprehensive collection of free online calculators that are fast, accurate, and respect your privacy.

I am enthusiastic about creating quality free tools for public use. Every calculator on this site was developed in-house using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I handle everything myself — coding, testing formulas, and maintaining the site.

Our Tools

The calculators on this site are grouped into several categories: financial, fitness & health, utilities, and productivity. All calculators were built from scratch.

More than 90% of the calculators are based on well-known formulas or equations from textbooks and established sources, such as:

  • The mortgage calculator uses standard amortization formulas
  • The BMI calculator follows WHO guidelines
  • The calorie calculator uses the Mifflin-St Jeor equation
  • The compound interest calculator uses standard financial formulas

If formulas vary by source or are controversial, I provide the results of all popular formulas so users can compare. Calculators meant for amusement (such as compatibility tests) are based on internal formulas and clearly labeled as such.

Accuracy & Verification

I take accuracy seriously. Before publishing any calculator:

  • I cross-check formulas against multiple reliable sources
  • I test edge cases with known inputs and expected outputs
  • I verify financial calculations against established calculators
  • I consult medical and financial references when building health and finance tools

Important disclaimer: While I strive for accuracy, these tools are for general reference only. For medical, financial, or legal decisions, always consult a qualified professional.

Privacy & Data

Your privacy is a core principle, not an afterthought:

  • All calculations happen locally in your browser
  • We do not collect, store, or transmit your personal data
  • No account registration is required to use any tool
  • No cookies are used for tracking purposes
  • We do not serve third-party advertisements

This site does not have servers processing your calculations. Your data never leaves your device.

Content & Licensing

All descriptive content on this site was written in-house. Where external references are used, they are cited and linked. The tools themselves are built using vanilla JavaScript with no external dependencies for core functionality.
